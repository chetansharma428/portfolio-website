About Me

Hello, everyone! I'm Chetan Sharma, a developer who finds joy in creating live projects that reach and impact both known and unknown audiences. My coding journey began during my BCA studies when I had my first encounter with the 'hello world' program in C language. From there, I delved into web development, building my very first basic HTML and CSS web page.
As time flew by, I pursued my MCA and in 2022, I had the incredible opportunity to intern at OpenWorkPark, a start-up. During my internship, I gained invaluable experience by working on real-world projects and applying my DSA and web development skills to solve complex problems. It was an enriching experience as I learned how to navigate and debug issues in large-scale projects, which were being utilized by thousands of users.
Recently, I've been actively working with various technologies, and my skillset includes proficiency in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Tailwind CSS, JavaScript, React, Angular, Node.js, MongoDB, SQL, and GitHub.
I'm always eager to take on new challenges and further expand my knowledge in the ever-evolving world of web development. I find immense satisfaction in turning ideas into reality and delivering innovative solutions to make a positive impact on people's lives.

Thank you for taking the time to learn a bit about me and my journey as a developer. I look forward to collaborating and creating exciting projects that leave a lasting impression on the world!
